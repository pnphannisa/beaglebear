Background

BEAGLE & BEAR is a quantitative and spatial analysis service provider founded by a group of professionals and researchers from various industries. Our past projects are ranged from word prediction machine, location of traffic incident visualization, stock market prediction model, and etc. Please see our sample projects. We offer a wide range of data analysis services, including but not limited to:

- Data Visualization
- Experimental Design, Hypothesis Testing aka A/B Tests
- Machine Learning Predictions with Regressions, Decision Trees, SVM, Ensemble Learning and More
- Automated Reports
- Data Product Prototyping
- Recommendation Engines
- Natural Language Processing and Sentimental Analysis
- Mapping and Geospatial Analysis
- Deep Learning

Our tools include but not limited to:

- Python, R, Scala
- SQL, Hive, Spark
- ArcGIS with Extensions

We are willing to cater to your preferences.
